# Background music

`lofi-bubbles.mp3` — "Bubbles ( Lofi , Bright , Relaxed )" by **HoliznaCC0**,
dedicated to the public domain under **CC0 1.0 Universal** (no attribution
required). Source: the *Public Domain Lofi* album on Free Music Archive
(https://freemusicarchive.org/music/holiznacc0/public-domain-lofi).

Trimmed to a 60-second loop with fade-in/out and re-encoded at 128 kbps for the
repo. Used as the default `video.music.path` in `config/shorts.yaml`, mixed
under narration at low volume.
